AQ215 AQUATIC PARK

General Information
Dimensions:
28'-11.24" x 25'-1.18" x 14'-7.98"
8.82m x 7.65m x 4.47m
Max fall height: 2'-11 7/16" - 0.9m
Safety surfacing area: 766 ft² - 71.17m²
Play capacity (users)/ Age group:
40 users / > 3 years
Weight: 1653.5 lb - 750kg
Installation time: 2 people / 24 hours
Materials - Appendix A
Screws: Stainless steel 304
Structure: 4 inch round 304 stainless steel pipe
Walls: HDPE
Accesories: Nylon
Slide: LDPE - Low density polyethylene
Platforms: Fiberglass reinforced polyester

The exciting 'Aquatic Anemone' game simulates the beauty of the marine world. Its tentacles, actually interactive water jets, challenge visitors to an aquatic adventure. A weighted bucket adds excitement with large water drops, while moving elements add intrigue. This unique experience combines fun and surprises in a captivating marine environment, offering an exciting aquatic adventure.

This product is designed under the standards required by the European safety standard for playgrounds (UNE-EN1176) and is manufactured with high quality and resistant materials, which allows for a long service life
