The exciting 'Aquatic Anemone' game
simulates the beauty of the marine world. Its
tentacles, actually interactive water jets,
challenge visitors to an aquatic adventure. A
weighted bucket adds excitement with large
water drops, while moving elements add
intrigue. This unique experience combines fun
and surprises in a captivating marine
environment, offering an exciting aquatic
adventure.

This product is designed under the standards
required by the European safety standard for
playgrounds (UNE-EN1176) and is
manufactured with high quality and resistant
materials, which allows for a long service life
